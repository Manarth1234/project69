Campus Life

School Library

Was established in 2002.

The school library is a tranquil and glorious place to visit. Entailing a wide variety of books, the library is an ideal place in the school to peacefully complete any homework or assignment. The library also hosts many cometitions such as the Chess Tournament and E-Sports tournament.

Table Tennis Area

Was established in 2003.

The table tennis area is a great place to play table tennis and also play activities that involve fundamental movement skills. The area also consists of benches allowing students to sit, eat, and do homework or assignments there.
